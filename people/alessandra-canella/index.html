Alessandra Canella

Design Leader & Mentor

Alessandra is a design leader with 10+ years shaping product and service design across startups, scale-ups, and the public sector. Formerly Head of Design at Cazoo, MyTutor, and TPXImpact, she's known for translating business goals into clear direction for Tech and Design teams to drive growth, retention, and efficiency. Founder of Mega Mentor and Service Design-in', she brings a collaborative, pragmatic, and user-centred approach to every challenge.
